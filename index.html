Aim for the attacker’s groin to cause intense pain and create an opportunity to escape.

Use Palm Strikes

👩‍⚖️ Girls' Rights in India

Every girl has the right to dignity, safety, and equality. Learn about your rights under laws like the POSH Act (2013) and PCR Act (1955).

🔐 Safety Strategies

Use safe travel habits, trust instincts, share locations, and avoid isolated spaces. Always keep emergency numbers handy.

📜 Important Laws to Know

IPC Section 354: Protects against assault or criminal force on women.
Section 509: Punishes words or gestures that insult women's modesty.
POCSO Act: Protects minors from sexual offense.
